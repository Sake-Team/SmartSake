Temperature Probes

Place the probe in a known reference (ice bath = 0°C, boiling water at sea level ≈ 100°C, or another trusted thermometer), wait for the reading to stabilise, type the reference temperature, and click Calibrate. The offset is constrained to ±5°C — anything larger usually means a wiring or probe issue rather than calibration drift.

Load Cells

Tare: clear the scale, click Tare. Calibrate: place a known weight, type its mass in kg, click Calibrate. The running scale picks up the new tare/factor automatically — no service restart needed.
